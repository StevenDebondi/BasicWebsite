Welcome to Spendity


Our goal is to help people manage their spending on mobile apps that have in-app purchase options.

If you have struggled with keeping control of your spending on mobile apps that include in-app purchases, then we are here to help you keep track of your spending and maintain control of your finances.

We want to help you realize how much you have been spending inside these apps and maintain a healthy spending habit going forwards.
We believe that by using our app to keep track of your spending habits, we can help you start saving money and begin your journey towards your monetary goals.
