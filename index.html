Welcome to Spendity


Welcome to Spendity, a startup dedicated to helping you track your in-app spending with ease and simplicity. Our team is passionate about making financial management accessible to everyone, and we believe that tracking your in-app spending is an important step towards achieving that goal.

At Spendity, we understand that it can be difficult to keep track of all the in-app purchases you make, especially when you use multiple apps across different devices. That's why we've developed a solution that makes it easy for you to see all your in-app spending in one place, no matter what app you're using.

Our app is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, so you don't need to be a financial expert to use it. With Spendity, you can easily track your spending habits, set budgets, and receive alerts when you're close to reaching your limits. We also offer detailed analytics to help you understand your spending patterns and make informed decisions about your finances.
